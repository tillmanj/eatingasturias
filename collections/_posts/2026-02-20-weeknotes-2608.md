## What's a weeknote?
Weeknotes are notes about what I have been doing this week (specifically concerning Eating Asturias --- for farming and personal weeknotes, see [my personal site](https://jontillman.me/now/)). They are a part of my plan to work in public with this project; to make the process transparent and open.

## Successes This Week
1. I have mostly wrestled FrontMatter CMS to the ground and made it do some helpful things for me. I am not a huge fan of how opaque its configuration is, but I've gotten it mostly working how I want. As a bonus, I now have a much more structured way to talk about the content on the site. See [Information Architecture](https://github.com/tillmanj/eatingasturias/wiki/Information-Architecture) on the wiki for more information.
