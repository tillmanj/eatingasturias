El Tenderete de Cabranes is a unique street market that takes place the second Sunday of every month in the village of Santolaya de Cabranes.

Combining a local artisan and farmers market with a second-hand swap meet, and usually with local musicians and/or a jam session, this market is a pretty interesting day out. Local crafts (many food related) dominate the market. There is a proud tradition of craft beer makers getting their start at El Tenderete, for instance. Bakers, canners, and food producers of all types also proudly display their wares alongside demonstration cooking, leather crafts, local farm produce, used books, and handmade crafts.

Second-hand clothing can often be found, as well as rummage sale style stalls. As the price to have a stall is very low, many people display stuff for sale only here and there, leading to a constant rotation of vendors.
